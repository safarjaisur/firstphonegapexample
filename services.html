Frequent Queries

Q: How does Innovaide protect our Intellectual Property and confidentiality?

Response: We identify all IPs and their respective owners (Client, Innovaide, or third parites), to be used in the program. Any new IP to be created gets typically assigned to Client unless otherwise agreed to by Client. We understand and appreciate the importance of confidentiality and IP because we create some for our own use as well as for our clients. We will discuss and meet all your requirements. We use technology and infrastructure to restrict the sharing of information on need basis.

Q. Does Innovaide have the technical ability to solve our problem?

Response: We have a team of very senior engineers who can engage with you at the architecture level to solve your problems. We’ll be happy to introduce you to our team.

Q. Does Innovaide have the global reach to provide the most optimized solution?

Response: Yes, with our multiple locations, we can meet your requirements from co-location to cost effective. Our processes and experience ensures that our geographically dispersed teams perform effectively.

Q: Does Innovaide have the capability to grow to service our needs?

Response: Yes. We have access to resources in all major technology hubs in US and India.

Q: How does Innovaide work with our internal resources?

Response: Innovaide seeks to maximize ROI for our clients by analyzing the available resources from client, Innovaide and third parties, and creating a plan to use them in an effective manner.

Q: How does Innovaide support us after the product release?

Response: Innovaide offers comprehensive support packages and tailors the support needed for each client and product.
